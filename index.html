INCT-CERBC Promotes the 23rd INFOCUS and 2nd ISHAM LATAM in Curitiba

INCT-CERBC is proud to promote the 23rd INFOCUS Congress and the 2nd ISHAM LATAM Congress, which will take place in Curitiba, Brazil, from July 22–25, 2026, at the Fiep Events Center.

Organized by Flavio Queiroz Telles, Vânia Aparecida Vicente (INCT-CERBC coordinator), and Arnaldo Lopes Colombo, INFOCUS is internationally recognized for its scientific quality, addressing key challenges in medical and veterinary mycology.

The event will include Educational Workshops and the Future Leader Campaign, sponsored by ACCORD.

Curitiba, the capital of Paraná, is recognized for its innovative urban planning and excellent infrastructure for scientific events.

Important dates:
Abstract submission: March 12 – May 29, 2026
Early Bird registration: until March 30, 2026

Discounts are available for ISHAM members and students.
More information: www.infocus2026.com
